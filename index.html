Los mejores tipos de café están acá

Arabica

Arabica

Arabica doesn't always taste the same. Coffe made from the upper-quality beans has an intense, intricate aroma that can be reminiscent of flowers, fruit, honey, chocalate, caramel, or toasted bread.

Robusta

Robusta

Robusta has a rubbery, bitter, grain-like flavor which lives a dry aftertaste. To harsher taste of Robusta is a great complement to Arabica, givin it depth, flavor, and crema.

Excelsa

Excelsa

In 2006 Excelsa was reclassified as a type of Liberica by Aaron P.Davis, a british botanist. It is used in combination with other coffees to add extra thickness and flavor. It has a mix of light and dark roast aroma.

Liberica

Liberica

Liberica has smokey aroma. In fact, some compare the taste of Liberica to "liquid tobacco". It has a slightly lower caffeine concentration than the two most popular coffee types.
